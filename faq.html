Packages & Add-Ons
Packages are pre-built event setups that combine the most common services and equipment. Individual products are better when you already know exactly what you need or want to build a custom setup piece by piece.
Yes. The booking flow is designed so you pick the package first, then add upgrades after. This keeps the package as the foundation while allowing you to customize the event.

Sound, Lighting & Equipment
Some sound setups include both tops and subs, while smaller sound options may include only QSC top speakers. The Products page separates these so clients can choose the right sound level for the event.
Yes. DJ rental options can include CDJ 3000 units, Pioneer DJM-900NXS2 mixer, XDJ-XZ, and a premium club mixer/CDJ package depending on availability.
Moving heads are professional event lights that move, rotate, and create beams, patterns, and motion effects. They make a setup feel more like a club, concert, or high-energy event.
Yes. The Products page includes moving head options with either totems or a goal post. Goal post setups are larger and usually cost more because they require more trussing and setup time.
Photo Booth, Photography & Video
Digital-only photo booth focuses on digital sharing and online delivery. Digital + print includes the digital experience plus printed photos at the event.
Yes. The photo booth includes props, and backdrop options may be available depending on the event setup and package selected.
Photography deliverables can be selected from 50 to 250 edited pictures, depending on the coverage option chosen.
Videography options range from a short 15-second event recap up to a full 1-minute recap, with options in between depending on the final deliverable you choose.
Venue, Setup & Event Requirements
Setup time depends on the package and venue access. Simple setups may need less time, while larger lighting, trussing, LED wall, cold spark, and photo booth setups need more time. Venue load-in access should be confirmed before the event.
Yes. All sound, lighting, DJ equipment, photo booth, and effects require power. Larger setups may need multiple reliable circuits. We can review the setup and let you know what power is needed before the event.
Outdoor events are possible, but weather protection, stable flooring, safe power, wind conditions, and venue rules matter. Some equipment may require covering or backup plans if the event is outside.
Yes. Effects such as cold sparks, fog, haze, or similar equipment may require venue approval and safe placement. Some venues restrict effects because of fire alarms, ceiling height, flooring, or house policies.
