Heat District Productions

Website Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 30, 2026

1. Overview

Heat District Productions respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you visit our website, request a quote, select packages or products, submit checkout details, or contact us directly.

By using this website, submitting a form, or providing event information to Heat District Productions, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide through our website forms, booking process, checkout page, email, phone, text, or social media messages.

  • Contact information: name, phone number, email address, and preferred contact method.
  • Event information: event date, event type, venue name, venue address, guest count, start time, setup notes, and special requests.
  • Booking selections: selected package, selected add-ons, product cart items, event hours, estimated totals, deposit amounts, and remaining balance.
  • Quote request details: services needed, custom production requests, budget-related notes, and event preferences.
  • Communication records: messages sent through our contact form, email, phone, Instagram, or other communication channels.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to respond to your inquiries, prepare quotes, review event details, create booking estimates, send invoices, coordinate event production, and communicate with you before and after your event.

  • To respond to quote requests, booking requests, and general questions.
  • To prepare event estimates, package recommendations, and custom production builds.
  • To create deposit invoices and send payment instructions.
  • To coordinate event logistics such as timing, location, setup needs, power requirements, and access details.
  • To improve our website, services, packages, and client experience.
  • To keep internal records related to quotes, bookings, invoices, and event communication.

4. Invoices, Deposits, and Payments

Heat District Productions may use third-party tools such as QuickBooks or other payment/invoicing platforms to create invoices, send payment links, collect deposits, and track payment status.

Our website does not directly collect or store your full credit card number, bank account details, or payment card security code. Payment information is handled by the payment or invoicing provider used to complete the transaction.

Deposit amounts shown on the website are estimates until reviewed and confirmed by Heat District Productions. Final pricing may change based on venue access, staffing, power needs, event complexity, travel, timing, and custom production requirements.

5. Website Storage and Session Data

Our website may temporarily store package selections, add-on selections, product cart items, estimated totals, and checkout details in your browser using session storage or similar browser-based storage. This helps the website remember your selections as you move from packages to add-ons to checkout.

This browser-based information is mainly used to make the booking flow work correctly. You can clear this data by closing the browser session, clearing your browser storage, or using the clear cart options where available.

6. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only when needed to operate our business, complete your booking, process invoices, respond to your request, or comply with legal requirements.

  • Service providers: invoicing, payment processing, contact forms, email services, hosting, website tools, and other business operations.
  • Event-related vendors: only when necessary for a specific event or custom production request.
  • Legal or safety reasons: if required to comply with law, enforce agreements, prevent fraud, or protect the rights and safety of Heat District Productions, our clients, or others.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information submitted to us. However, no website, email system, payment platform, or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Please avoid sending highly sensitive payment information, passwords, or private documents through regular contact forms, text messages, or social media messages.

8. Your Choices and Requests

You may contact us to request that we update, correct, or delete certain personal information we have collected from you, subject to any business, legal, accounting, or contractual obligations that require us to keep certain records.

You may also choose not to provide certain information, but some information may be required for us to prepare a quote, confirm event details, create an invoice, or complete a booking.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are intended for clients, event organizers, and individuals who are able to request event services. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

Heat District Productions may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, the updated date at the top of this page may be revised. Continued use of the website after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, contact Heat District Productions directly.
