What We Handle

Creative. Technical. Client-Focused.

Our setup is built around a simple idea: the event should look good, sound good, feel good, and be easy for the client to understand.

01
Lighting & Atmosphere
We focus on lighting placement, color, movement, effects, and the overall visual atmosphere that makes the event feel elevated.
02
Photography & Content
We capture the people, energy, details, and key moments so the event has a visual story beyond the night itself.
03
DJ & Event Flow
Music, timing, transitions, and crowd energy are handled with the goal of keeping the event moving naturally.
04
Design & Client Experience
From package direction to setup presentation, we care about making the process clear and the final result look intentional.

Our Process

From Inquiry to Event Day

This gives clients confidence by showing that there is a clear process behind the production.

Step 01
Understand the Event
We look at the event type, venue, date, guest count, timing, and the kind of energy the client wants.
Step 02
Build the Setup
The client chooses a package or builds a custom cart with sound, lighting, media, effects, and add-ons.
Step 03
Confirm the Details
We confirm timing, setup needs, access, power, design direction, and any special requests before event day.
Step 04
Execute the Experience
Ozzy and Julian handle the production, visuals, music, and client-facing details that bring the event together.
